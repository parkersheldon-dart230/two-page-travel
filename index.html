  • Explore Buckingham Palace, one of the most iconic and impressive buildings in the world, it's a must-visit to experience the best of British history and culture Source

So many things to do in Tokyo

  • Explore the tallest mountain in the world, Mount Fuji
  • Explore the Ghibli Museum and see your favorite Studio Ghibli films come to life
  • Go up the Tokyo Tower and oversee the many wonderful views of Tokyo

So many things to do in Rome

  • Explore the many monuments of Rome like the Colosseum and the Trevi Fountain
  • Watch one of the most storied teams in Italy with AS Roma
  • Eat the very many foods that Italy has to offer, with Carbonara being one of the hottest in Italy

So many things to do in Barcelona

  • Explore the beautiful city of art and history in Barcelona
  • Watch one of the best teams in the world in FC Barcelona
  • Try the very many foods of Barcelona, with Paella being one of the finest dishes in Spain

So many things to do in Dublin

  • Have a good time at Temple Bar, with live music and drinks galore

  • Visit Trinity College Dublin, and get a tour of the oldest college in Ireland
